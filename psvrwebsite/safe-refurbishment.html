Need a safe refurbished?

Through its Melbourne based, professionally operated refurbishment centre, Professional Safe and Vault Removals offers a specialised refurbishment service for your high grade commercial safes and vaults.

Our team of highly qualified and industry trained safes fabricators with over 30 years experience have the expertise to restore your old safe and vault to its original glory. This is a unique niche, highly customised service that we provide on a project basis to our valued clients such as, antique collectors and jewellers, etc. We offer a complete turnkey project methodology commencing with assessing your specific restoration requirements, preparing a proposal covering aspects like colour scheme, quality of paint, temporary secure storage of existing contents, pre-restoration pick-up, post restoration delivery and install, etc.
